Real Life Images on a 3D Cube

Explanation This experiment is almost the same as the previous experiment.
The only difference here is the Images are real time images and need to be manipulated to get a good UI. The major application of this effect is done in Designing a e-Album or a website on photos. 


#cube .front{
background:white url("../../images/Back1.jpg") center center no-repeat;
-webkit-transform:translateZ(100px);
}
#cube .back{
background:white url("../../images/Back2.jpg") center center no-repeat;
-webkit-transform:rotateY(180deg) translateZ(100px);
}
